1 PASTE YOUR GOOGLE SHEETS DATA
📋 How to copy from Google Sheets:
1. Open your vocabulary sheet
2. Click on the first cell (A1)
3. Press Ctrl+Shift+End to select all rows
4. Press Ctrl+C to copy
5. Come back here and paste with Ctrl+V

4 IMPORT INTO QUIZLET — STEP BY STEP
1
Click "Copy all" above — the text is copied to your clipboard.
2
Open quizlet.com in a new tab and sign in to your account.
3
Click "+" in the top right corner and select "Create a study set".
4
On the creation page, click the "Import" link.
Create a new study set + Import →
5
In the popup, paste the text with Ctrl+V.
Your vocabulary will appear in the text box.
6
Set the separators exactly like this:
Between term and def. ✔ Tab
Between cards ✔ New line
⚠️ Wrong separators will break the import — make sure both are set correctly.
7
Click "Import" → Quizlet creates all your flashcards automatically. 🎉
You can then add a title to your set and save.
